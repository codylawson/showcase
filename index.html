My Name is Cody Lawson, I’m a User Interface Engineer with over 5 years of experience creating beautiful and easy to use applications for the web. I love data visualization and creating animations that tell a story as well as inform.

01. Experience and Skills

As a User Interface Engineer at Esri I worked on dozens of web applications for companies like Walmart, Shell, and PG&E, as well as government agencies like the National Geospatial Agency (NGA) and the Department of Energy (DOE).

Projects ranged from relatively minor engagements with just a few people and lasting a couple months, to larger efforts with multiple scrum teams developing for two or more years.

I have experience with modern web technologies such as jQuery, Backbone and Underscore, Dojo, Tooling (Grunt & Gulp), and SCSS.

02. Sample Project: Guy Carpenter

Never heard of Guy Carpenter? They’re the industry leader for reinsurance, the practice of splitting risk across multiple companies so that when the next Hurricane Sandy hits New York one company isn’t left with the bill to renovate the Empire State building.

AdvantagePoint allows analysts and actuaries to monitor their properties and see how they might be affected by catostrophic events.
